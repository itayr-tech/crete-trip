Wednesday · Jun 17
Arrival & first evening
~1h 50min drive from HER
4–5 pm
✈️

Land at Heraklion (HER)

Pick up rental car. Head west on E75 toward Rethymno, then south through the mountains via Spili. Winding but well-paved and beautiful.

~6:45 pm
🏨

Check in at AMÉNTE

Drop bags, settle in. The property has Libyan Sea views — take 20 minutes just sitting with it before anything else.

8:30 pm
🍽️

Dinner at Taverna Plateia, Mirthios village

5 min drive up the mountain. Village square, panoramic bay view, honest Cretan food. Order saganaki, grilled fish.

No reservation needed · arrive by 8:30 for the golden light
Thursday · Jun 18
Kourtaliotiko Gorge → Preveli Beach
Big day — start early
7:30 am
☀️

Early breakfast & pack

Bring 2L water each, water shoes, a light layer for the gorge.

8:00 am
🏔️

Kourtaliotiko Gorge hike

Trailhead near Asomatos village (~20 min drive). 3–4 hrs through limestone walls, waterfalls, and cold river pools. Moderate difficulty — some scrambling and cold-water crossings. Water shoes essential.

8am start beats the tour buses by 2+ hours
~noon
🏖️

Preveli Beach & lagoon swim

Palm-fringed river lagoon meets the Libyan Sea between dramatic limestone cliffs. Swim in both the lagoon and the sea. Picnic or grab food from the beach kiosk.

2:30 pm
⛪

Preveli Monastery (optional)

17th-century monastery above the gorge, 15 min from the beach. Worth 30 minutes if you have energy left.

8:00 pm
🍽️

Dinner at Vrisi Restaurant, Mirthios

Best cooking in the area. Modern Cretan, local farmers, beautifully executed. Must-order: lamb in paprika sauce, shrimp orzo, zucchini flowers.

Reserve in advance — fills up fast
Friday · Jun 19
Skinaria snorkeling + Sfakia & Loutro boat
Varied pace
9:00 am
🤿

Skinaria Beach — snorkeling

8 km from AMÉNTE (~12 min). 30m+ water visibility, best snorkeling near Plakias. Very few crowds. Taverna for lunch.

Premium sunbeds ~€25 · worth it for shade
1:30 pm
🚗

Drive to Sfakia (~40 min west)

Small fishing village on the Libyan Sea. Pick up the short ferry to Loutro — runs frequently in summer (~10 min crossing).

2:30 pm
⛵

Loutro — swim, wander, coffee

One of the most peaceful spots in Crete. No roads, no cars, just whitewashed houses on the water. Swim off the rocks, sit at a waterfront cafe. Take the ferry back by 5pm.

6:30 pm
🌅

Sunset at Souda Beach

Sheltered horseshoe cove with the best golden-hour light in the area. 7 min from AMÉNTE.

8:30 pm
🍽️

Dinner at Alatsi, Plakias

Best cooking in Plakias village. Order: grilled octopus, chicken stuffed with Cretan graviera and apaki, seafood linguine.

Reserve ahead · walkable from AMÉNTE
Saturday · Jun 20
Frangokastello + coastal hike + last proper dinner
Relaxed pace
9:00 am
🏰

Drive to Frangokastello (~35 min west)

14th-century Venetian castle on a beach. Adjacent beach (Orthi Ammos — sand dunes) is one of Crete's most beautiful and rarely crowded.

11:30 am
🐟

Lunch in Sfakia village

Small fishing village on the water. Pick any harbor fish taverna — all serve fresh catch. Lambs on the spit on weekends.

3:00 pm
🥾

Coastal path: Plakias → Damnoni Beach

1.5–2 hours, easy. Follows the coastline with Libyan Sea views the whole way. Swim at Damnoni before driving back.

7:00 pm
🌄

Mirthios loop walk & sunset

Walk up to the hilltop village above Plakias (45 min, easy). Panoramic views of the entire bay. Watch the sunset from the ridge.

8:30 pm
🍽️

Last dinner: Taverna Dionyssos, Mirthios

Most traditional of the Mirthios restaurants. Family-run, pick-from-the-pot Cretan cooking. Order: goat with artichokes, snails, aubergine casserole.

Sunday · Jun 21
Kotsifos Gorge + checkout + drive north
Checkout by noon
7:00 am
🏔️

Kotsifos Gorge hike

Trailhead in Kanevos village (~15 min from AMÉNTE). 2 hours, moderate. Gorge narrows to 10m with 600m vertical walls, a chapel carved into the rock face, and a 20m waterfall. Dry riverbed in June — easy walking. Back by 9:30am.

9:30 am
🧳

Shower, pack, checkout

Settle the bill. Slow final coffee before hitting the road.

11:30 am
🚗

Drive back to Heraklion airport (~1h 50min)

Stop in Spili village (~30 min into the drive) — small mountain village with a famous lion-head fountain. Good for a last coffee and final view of the Cretan interior.

Before you go

📋 To do

  • Book rental car at Heraklion airport
  • Health / travel insurance
  • Alatsi: reserve for Friday dinner
  • Preveli Beach: go before 10am or after 4pm
  • Loutro ferry: departs Sfakia, every ~1–2 hrs, ~€5 each way

🎒 Pack

  • Goggles (correction lenses)
  • Sunglasses
  • Dry bag
  • Driver's license
  • Water shoes (gorge crossings)
  • 2L water bottles each
  • Light layer for gorge shade

🚗 On the road

  • Route: Heraklion → E75 west → Rethymno → south via Spili (stop both ways)
  • Car parking: easy everywhere outside Rethymno old town center
  • Skinaria: bring your own mask if you have one
